Discover our innovative range of solar structures, designed to seamlessly integrate with your home’s unique style and architecture. We offer a variety of advanced solutions to suit diverse needs.

Y-Shaped Solar Carport Structure

This design is commonly used in solar carport installations where solar panels are mounted on an elevated frame that provides shade for vehicles while simultaneously generating solar energy. The "Y-shaped" central support column allows for a more open space beneath the structure, making it suitable for areas with vehicle traffic or parking needs. This type of design is efficient for maximizing the use of space while integrating solar technology seamlessly.

Ground Mounted Solar Structure

Sturdy, efficient, and built to last—our ground-mounted solar structures are designed for optimal energy generation. Engineered for durability and ease of installation, they’re the perfect foundation for your solar power projects.

Pole Carport Structure

Maximize space with our Pole solar carport structure — combining durability, functionality, and clean energy generation. Ideal for parking areas, it offers reliable shelter while harnessing the power of the sun.

Rooftop Solar Mounting Structure

The structure with a triangular, raised frame. This type of setup is often used to secure solar panels on flat or slightly inclined rooftops, optimizing the angle for maximum sunlight exposure while maintaining stability. The elevated frame also allows for airflow underneath the panels, which helps with cooling and efficiency. This specific design appears to utilize a concrete ballast system for anchoring without penetrating the roof surface, a common method for installations that need to avoid direct attachments to the roof.
